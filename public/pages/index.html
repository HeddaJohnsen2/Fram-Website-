This is how it works

Order groceries

Farm is a circular service that ensures you kitchen is always stocked with the essentials you need. Browse our sesonal selection, choose your items, and place your online order.

See our stock here

Stock up

Once you order arrives, unpack the produce and store it however it works best for you. Hold on to the containers- they're yours to keep until your next order.

Scan & reorder

Ready for a refill? Grab the container from your last order and scan the QR code with our app or your phone's camera. This instantly adds the items back to your cart, making reordering a breeze.

Swap and reuse

When we deliver your new order, simply leave your empty containers outside. We'll pick them up, clean them thoroughly, and reuse them for future deliveries
